About Bridan Design Build

Building Dreams. Designing Futures. Creating Landmarks.

Kenya’s Trusted Partner in Architecture, Construction & Interior Design

Bridan Design Build is a leading, fully registered and certified architecture, building, and interior design company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in delivering innovative, sustainable, and client-focused solutions for residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects.

Our multidisciplinary team of architects, engineers, interior designers, and project managers brings together creativity, technical expertise, and a passion for excellence. From concept to completion, we handle every stage—site analysis, architectural planning, approvals, construction, interior fit-out, and finishing.

  • Registered Architect (BORAQS) & NCA Certified Contractor
  • Turnkey design & build solutions
  • Modern, sustainable, and energy-efficient designs
  • Transparent project management & cost control
  • Personalized service and collaborative approach
  • Aftercare, maintenance, and renovation expertise

We believe every space should tell a story—your story. Whether you’re building your dream home, developing apartments, or upgrading your office, Bridan delivers quality, innovation, and peace of mind.

Our Services

Custom designed AND comprehensive design solutions for every space.

Residential Design

Personalized home transformations balancing aesthetics and functionality. From initial concept to final installation, we handle every detail, ensuring a cohesive and beautiful living space.

Commercial Design

Inspiring workspaces that enhance productivity and brand identity. We create environments that foster creativity, collaboration, and a positive employee experience.

Space Planning

Optimized floor plans for maximum space efficiency and flow. We analyze your needs and develop layouts that maximize usability and create a comfortable and functional environment.

Custom Furniture

Bespoke furniture designed to perfectly complement your space. We work with skilled artisans to create unique pieces tailored to your specific style and requirements.

Color Consultation

Expert guidance in selecting the perfect color palette to evoke the desired mood and enhance your space. We consider lighting, existing décor, and your personal preferences to create a harmonious and visually appealing environment.

Renovation & Remodeling

Complete renovation and remodeling services, from kitchen and bath updates to whole-house transformations. We manage every stage of the project, ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience.

Lighting Design

Strategic lighting solutions to enhance ambiance, functionality, and energy efficiency. We create layered lighting schemes that highlight architectural features and create a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Interior Styling & Decoration

Adding the finishing touches that bring your space to life. We select and arrange furniture, accessories, and artwork to create a cohesive and personalized environment.
