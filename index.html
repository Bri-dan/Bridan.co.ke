Why We Stand Out As The Best House Designer in Kenya.

We at Bridan Design Build are experts in our field. We design houses and construct them, along with doing home renovation projects. We are qualified to provide contracting services by the National Construction Authority (NCA). We are also qualified design architects. Our team includes a BORAQS Registered Architect. We are a vibrant company, formed during the COVID-19 crisis in 2020 and as of Sep 2024 will have worked on projects worth a combined value of Ksh 100 million+. Our construction team is able to guarantee: quality workmanship, project delivery within agreed timeline, minimal construction budget variations (10% or less).
